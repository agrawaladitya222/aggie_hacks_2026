An obvious question here might be, how are the hidden gems detected and how is Mission ROI calculated?
Lets start with Mission ROI. We first find the Annual Spending on Programs for that org (which is taken 
directly from the 990 form).
We then divide this amount by the calculated donation needed to stabilize (how much money is needed to get the org to 
6 months of operating reserves) (the monthly operated cost is calculated by dividing Total Yearly Expenses by 12).
If this organization were to go under, we would lose all the programs that they host/contribute to. So by helping an
org reach their 6 month reserve, you are effectively helping them ensure that their programs will continue. 


However, as you can see, Mission ROI is not the sole factor that determines the cost efficiency score that we calculate.
